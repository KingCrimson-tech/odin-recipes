Salad

Description

This apple pecan salad with a simple homemade vinaigrette has crunch, sweetness, and a salty kick.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Shallot
  • Ground Pepper

Steps

  1. Combine vinegar, Dijon, shallots, salt, and pepper in a small bowl. Whisk in olive oil until dressing starts to emulsify.
  2. Combine mixed greens, apple, pecans, cranberries, and bacon in a large bowl and toss. Drizzle as much dressing over the salad as you like, and toss again. Sprinkle with feta, and serve.
